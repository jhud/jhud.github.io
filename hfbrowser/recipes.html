Caramel Dumplings

batter

  • 1 1/4 cup SR flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • pinch salt
  • 1 tspn vanilla essence
  • 30g butter
  • 1/3 cup milk

sauce

  • 30g butter
  • pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  1. Combine all batter ingredients in bowl and stir until smooth.
  2. Place all sauce ingredients in medium saucepan and bring to simmer.
  3. Add spoonfuls of batter to the sauce and keep on low heat for about 30 min or until dumplings have solidified.
  4. Have with icecream and/or cream.
Moroccan Curry

First lot

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 or 2 chopped chillis
  • 1/2 tsp paprika

Second lot

  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 4 cups cubed sweet potato
  • 3 cups cubed eggplant
  • 1 capsicum
  • 4 cups zucchini
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1/2 cup raisins or sultanas

Third lot

  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • couscous
  1. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil for 2 min.
  2. Add spices and stir in.
  3. Add veges in given order, saute each one until it changes colour slightly.
  4. Stir in raisins and beans, add a bit of water or tomato juice if it is too dry. Simmer until soft.
  5. Add parsley just before it is ready.
  6. Serve with couscous. To cook the couscous, follow directions on the packet, and don't stir it while it is absorbing the water. Add a knob of butter if desired.
