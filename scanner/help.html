Help
  1. Point the camera at your document.
  2. Play with the White Level slider until the white paper looks completely white and the text is readable.
  3. Press the capture button.
  4. Wait until the spinner stops.
  5. Scans can be found in the "Scans" folder in your photos.
Controls

Controls paper brightness. Move to the right to make the paper brighter. Scans look and print best when the paper background is completely white.

Troubleshooting
I can't find the the "Scans" folder or my photos!

You need to give the app access to your photos. Go to Settings/Data Protection/Photos and turn on the Nude Scan switch.
