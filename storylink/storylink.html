StoryLink - a journalism service project being developed by Sarah and James Hudson

To our knowledge, there is no centralised, popular, reliable and indexed database for citizen journalism -- a "People's Reuters", the "Airbnb of Storytelling".

While the media struggles with expectations that it has eyes and ears all around the world as stories break, there are over 2 billion smartphone users worldwide -- including many who are vulnerable, displaced or who go voiceless. Smartphones are emerging as key to the democratisation of media, but apps and services must be accessible, simple and attractive.

We are creating a user-friendly mobile app that allows the secure, straightforward uploading of tagged, geographically or otherwise, categorised content by registered users. Content is user-reviewed and ranked for quality and appeal, allowing contributors to build their credibility and digital reputation.

The result is a database of citizen-produced raw footage, interviews and fully fledged stories accessible for download and use by media professionals, for example, journalists or NGOs.

Please write to us!
